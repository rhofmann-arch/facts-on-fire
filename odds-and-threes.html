Odds & Threes
Score points by making odd numbers or numbers divisible by 3 from your two cards. Add, subtract, multiply, divide — or place the digits side by side.

How to play
Setup

Cards A–10, no face cards. Ace = 1. Two modes available.

Practice Round — scaffolded

All possible results are shown as chips. Pick one. Green chips score a point. Good for introducing the game.

5-Round Game — competitive
  • Each team is dealt two cards. No options are shown.
  • Type a number you can make from those cards (+, −, ×, ÷, or place digits together).
  • Score 1 point if the number is odd or divisible by 3.
  • Correct? Point awarded — enter another, or Pass to bank points and end your turn.
  • Wrong (not achievable, not scoring, or already entered)? Turn over.
  • Find every scoring answer and your turn ends automatically.
Example — cards 4 and 6
4664 4+6=106−4=2 4×6=24 ✓

Only 24 scores — divisible by 3. Entering 10 or 2 would end the turn.
