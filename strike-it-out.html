⚡ How to play
Setup

A number line from 0 to 20. Two teams take turns.

Team 1 — first turn only

Pick any two numbers, cross them both out, then circle either their sum or their difference. Circled numbers can still be used once more.

All other turns

You must use the circled number. Pick one more available number, cross both out, then circle the sum or difference.

Winning

The winner is the team that stops their opponent from being able to take a turn.

Cooperative challenge

Switch to Cooperative mode and work together to use as many numbers as possible. Can you use all 21?
